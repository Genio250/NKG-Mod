A mod that keeps your lifeblood when sitting at a bench.

The mod will keep any lifeblood you have after you sit, useful for pantheon runs if you keep lifeblood from previous rooms, or for randos.

Lifeblood heart and core will act as +2 and +4 masks respectively.
If before benching you have the charm equipped and less lifeblood than it gives, it will refresh to the buff of the charm, but unequipping it will lose those masks.

As of 1.0 the mod doesn't support Joni's Blessing. Equipping it will revert lifeblood to vanilla behavior.
